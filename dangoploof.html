Experience

Crochet is a very important passion to me, so I created Dango Ploof, a way to document my journey and sell my creations.


I started crocheting in 2020, and I am glad to say I feel like I have come a long way since then. I am happy to have those around me that enjoy and support what I do. Because of them, I have been able to spread the love and sell my crochets to 4 different countries as well as make some money to supply my hobby.

To create a central hub for all my crochet endeavors, I used React.js and created a website display my works and organize commissions!


To learn more and look at some of my crochets, take a look at my website! (ouo)
