Quantum


  • Category: Game
  • Made with C# and Unity
  • Itch Page: https://allisanlu.itch.io/quantum

Experience

Quantum is a 2 player co-op platformer that can be played splitscreen or networked.

This game was created as a project with VGDev, the video game development club at Georgia Tech. For this project, my friend and I led a team of about 30 artists, musicians, designers, and programmers to collaborate and bring our vision for this game to life. Set in a post-apocalyptic world facing a multiverse catastrophe, this game is a platformer with physics based off Celeste mechanics with an extra ability to Quantum Lock and transfer momentum between players.

The most challenging part of this game for me was learning to do networking so that different devices could connect to one another and play the game together. I have never done anything related to networking before, and I've learned a lot about how actions between the host and clients need to be communicated to keep the game in sync.
