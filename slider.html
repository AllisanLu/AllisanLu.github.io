Experience

Slider is a single player puzzle game.


During my summer of 2022, I started assisting with art in the game Slider. My main job on the team was to create characters for 9 different regions based on the regional themes and the overall plot of the story.

I expanded beyond character creations at the end of 2022. Around then, I began working on programming mechanics for one of the 9 main areas/levels within the game. For that I have utilized graphs and graph algorithms like graph coloring to create a complex shape creation factory within the Jungle area of the game. Beyond that, I have also been implementing the level design for the area and placing puzzles.

Slider has released on Steam in 2024 with many positive reviews! You can check it out on Steam as well.
